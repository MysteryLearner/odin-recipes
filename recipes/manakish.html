Zaatar Manakish Recipe

Mediterranean flatbread that is made with a simple dough recipe and topped with an olive-oil based zaatar spread.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Olive Oil
  • Zaatar
  • Baking Powder

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients together and mix well to form a slighty sticky dough.
  2. Add oil to dough and knead until smooth.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rest for at least an hour.
  4. After an hour, take out dough and roll it into small circular pieces.
  5. Add a mixture of zaatar adn olive oil apply it to the dough.
  6. Preheat the oven.
  7. Put dough in oven and let it bake at 180 degress celcius for 15 min.
  8. Take out the manakish. Let it cool.
