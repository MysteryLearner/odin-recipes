Cinnamon Roll Recipe

A pillowy, soft baked treat that is tradionally filled with cinnamon and usually topped with an icing of some sort.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Yeast
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Baking Powder

Steps

  1. Add dry ingredients, including yeast, together and mix well.
  2. Add milk and butter, which should be melted, together.
  3. Mix dry and wet ingredients to form a slighty sticky dough.
  4. Add oil to dough and knead until smooth.
  5. Cover the dough and let it rest for at least an hour.
  6. After an hour, take out dough and roll it into a rectangle.
  7. Add a mixture of sugar, cinnamon and butter and apply it to the whole dough.
  8. Roll the dough and cut it into equal parts.
  9. Proof the dough for at least 20 min and preheat oven in the meantime.
  10. Put dough in oven and let it bake at 180 degress celcius for 25 min.
  11. Take out cinnamon rolls. Let it cool and serve with icing.
