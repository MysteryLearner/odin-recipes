Churros Recipe

A fried piece of dough usually served with chocolate.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Water
  • Butter
  • Cinnamon Powder

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients, except flour, to a pot and turn on heat.
  2. When mixture boils, add all the flour and stir well until a dough is formed.
  3. Let dough cool down, then roll it into equally-shaped pieces.
  4. Heat oil in a saucepan .
  5. Fry the dough until golden brown.
  6. Serve it warm with tea or chocolate.
